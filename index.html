Key Capabilities

Dashboard and Overview

  • Overall financial summary with income, expenses, and net balance.
  • Breakdowns by business/personal categories.
  • Quick stats: weekly/monthly expenses, pending invoices, tax deadlines.
  • Cards for receipt management, tax estimates, invoices, and mileage.
  • Filtering by business, personal, or all.
  • Visual budget progress and tax payment indicators.

Transactions Management

  • Add/edit transactions with amount, date, merchant, notes, category, and receipts.
  • List, search, sort, and filter by finance type (business/personal).
  • Auto/manual categorization with tax-deductible flags.
  • Intelligent matching to receipts or bank imports.
  • Recurring transactions: create, edit, list, and auto-generate (e.g., monthly rent).
  • Flag uncategorized or duplicates.
  • Income vs. expense classification with formatted displays.

Receipts and Scanning

  • Scan receipts via camera/photo library with OCR for data extraction.
  • Auto-categorization, confidence scoring, and tax deduction suggestions.
  • Split receipts between business/personal (percentage or line items).
  • Match to transactions or mark as cash.
  • View/zoom/edit receipt images with gestures.
  • Flag for review and provide insights.
  • Secure image storage and management.

Budgets Management

  • Create/edit budgets by month, category, and finance type (envelope, simple, zero-based, percentage).
  • List with progress tracking (spent vs. planned).
  • Monthly rollover for unused amounts.
  • Warnings for nearing limits (e.g., 80% threshold).
  • Integration with recurring transactions.

Invoices and Client Management

  • Create invoices with auto-numbers, clients, items, taxes, discounts.
  • Edit/view details, status (draft, sent, paid, overdue), payments.
  • Mark paid/partially paid; create linked transactions.
  • Send/track reminders for overdue invoices.
  • List/filter by status; search functionality.
  • Aging reports with charts.
  • Manage clients: add/edit/delete with contacts, terms, stats (invoiced, paid, reliability).

Mileage Tracking

  • Real-time auto-tracking with start/stop and background persistence.
  • Manual entry with addresses, distance, purpose.
  • IRS deductions based on rates (e.g., 2025: $0.67/mile).
  • List/filter by period; stats for miles, deductions.
  • View details with maps/routes; export CSV.
  • Recover incomplete trips.
  • Diagnostics for issues (permissions, battery tips).

Taxes and Deductions

  • Quarterly estimates using 2025 IRS brackets/deductions.
  • Configure filing status, state rates, safe harbor.
  • Flag deductions from receipts/transactions.
  • Notifications for deadlines/payments.
  • Year-end summaries with categories/savings estimates.

Reports and Exports

  • Reports with charts: income/expenses, budgets, categories, periods.
  • Export CSV/PDF (transactions, budgets, invoices, mileage) RFC 4180 compliant.
  • Share via system sheet with previews.

Settings and Security

  • Biometric/passcode protection (4/6-digit, Face ID/Touch ID).
  • Preferences: currencies, budget types, invoice terms, notifications.
  • Data export/import for backups.
  • App info, version, features.
  • Dark mode, accessibility, localization.

Notifications and Automation

  • Push for taxes, invoices, recurrings, budgets.
  • Background tasks for checks (reminders, instances).
  • Widgets for quick stats/timers.

General App Features

  • Secure SwiftData storage with app groups.
  • Business/personal separation across features.
  • Error handling with feedback.
  • Previews/diagnostics for dev/testing.
  • Logging, concurrency safety, optimizations.
